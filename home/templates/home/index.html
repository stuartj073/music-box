Music Box

Music box is essentially a mixture of two things. First and foremost, it is a site for musicians from all skill levels that wish to purchase musical equipment to enjoy and further their skill levels.
Secondly, it is an online meeting place for those with a musical interest to share with others their insights into a particular area of music in which they have interest in.
Finally, music box members can also sell their own equipment they are currently finished with and even their own vinyl records.
